04 · Common questions

The "so wait, how does this work?" list.

If you don't see your question, write to us. A human reads everything.

Can I switch plans?

Any time, in either direction. Upgrade and the new features unlock immediately, prorated. Downgrade and the change applies at the next billing cycle — no surprise charges.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access through the end of your current billing period, then your account becomes read-only. Your manuscripts stay where they are; you can re-subscribe at any time and pick up exactly where you left off, or export everything and walk away.

Do you offer student or hardship discounts?

Yes. Verified students and writers in financial hardship can apply for a 50% discount on Starter and Pro. Write to us with a few sentences about your situation — we don't ask for documentation we wouldn't want to share ourselves.

Is there a free trial on the Team plan?

Team plans start with a 30-day pilot for the whole room, on us. Schedule a 20-minute onboarding call and we'll set up your collaborators, shared bibles, and SSO before the meeting ends.

Do unused report quotas roll over?

Unused reports roll over for one billing cycle, up to your plan's monthly limit. Beyond that, additional reports can be purchased à la carte — see the report packs above.

What if I just want one report and not a subscription?

Buy a report pack à la carte. The single report is $19, the three-pack is $45, and the pre-publication bundle (with line edit) is $129. No subscription, no recurring charge.

Are my manuscripts private?

Always. Your work is encrypted at rest and in transit, never used for AI training, and never shown to other users. The Beta Reader panel reads your draft, returns the report, and the content is purged from active memory.

Which currencies do you support?

USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, and TWD at launch. Local currency is auto-detected at checkout. VAT and sales tax are included where required.

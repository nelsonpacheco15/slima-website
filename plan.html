04 · Common questions

The "so wait, how does this work?" list.

If you don't see your question, write to us. A human reads everything.

What's included in the free plan?

Both Writing Studio and Script Studio with full editing features, habit tracking, version control, cloud sync, offline mode, and multi-format export.

What happens after the 7-day AI trial?

Your studios and all data remain intact. You keep full editing, sync, and export features. AI coaching features pause until you subscribe or use credits.

What's the difference between subscriptions and report packs?

Subscriptions include AI coaching, analysis tools, and monthly report credits. Report packs are one-time purchases for AI editor reports only.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from account settings. Your features continue until the billing period ends. Report packs never expire regardless.

What payment methods do you support?

Stripe processes all payments: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.
